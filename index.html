The Process

How it’s made
  1. 01

    Conceive

    Every piece begins with a feeling — not a prompt. I spend time defining the emotional core: the mood, texture, and atmosphere before a single word is typed into a generator.

    Ideation
  2. 02

    Direct

    I treat AI tools like a cinematographer treats a camera — with intention. Hundreds of iterations and creative decisions go into each final frame. The AI doesn’t decide; I do.

    Generation
  3. 03

    Refine

    The real work happens in post. Compositing, color grading, pacing, and sound design transform raw generations into cinematic experiences that feel intentional from first frame to last.

    Post Production

About

I don’t generate videos.
I direct experiences, narratives, worlds.

There’s a craft to working with generative AI that most people miss. It’s not about typing a prompt and accepting what comes out — it’s about iterating, guiding, and knowing exactly when the machine has surprised you with something better than you planned.

My work lives at the intersection of cinema, design, and machine intelligence. Each piece starts with a feeling, a texture, or a half-formed idea — then the AI becomes a collaborator.

The result is a body of work that couldn’t exist without both human vision and artificial imagination working together.
